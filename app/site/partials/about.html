The Story

The government gave Canadians LegisInfo. It allows you to track bills through parliament and the Senate

It allows Canadians to read bills as they go through Parliament and the Senate.

It’s a good idea, but, we felt there was something missing.

Interaction!

How can we popularize the law, make it interactive, and not so... boring read? Bill Watch!

Now Canuks can share, debate, vote, and read Bills.

Why not give Canadians more tools that will help them keep up with government?

We built Citizen of Canada. The plan is to keep collecting data we find useful or interesting and present it to Canadians.

Now, we’re keeing busy thinking up more ways for Canadians to track government and be heard. Any suggestions? Dis tu!
