Privacy Statement

Citizens of Canada is a group of people that like to analyze politics, economics, and well, other people. Users can view, share and discuss the tools we use to study things and the results they bring.

Hello this is CC’s privacy statement, it outlines what information we collect from our visitors, who sees it, and what we use that information for. This is an important read so please do.

What personal information do you collect about me?

Here’s a list of the information we collect about you, your:

  • Name
  • Postal Code
  • Phone Number
  • Email Address
  • Date of Birth
  • Gender
  • Opinions
  • Hobbies and Interests
When you visit us, we also collect:
  • Information about your computer, including your IP address, the type of operation system and browser you use
  • What pages you visit on our site and what links you click on
  • What other sites you’re visited recently
How do you use this information?

The main reasons we collect personal information from you are:

  • Surveys
  • Customer service
  • Research

If it is necessary to complete these transactions, we may disclose your information to another company. For example, there are many companies that provide survey software. We may share information to with such a company in order to conduct a survey for research on a particular subject.

Use of Person Information for Secondary Reasons:

We may also use your personal information for other, secondary reasons, including:

  • Marketing
Sharing of Personal Information with Third Parties:

Sometimes we may also share your personal information with other companies, including:

  • Google
